AI iMessage Auto-Reply for Sales on Mac

Acknowledge simple questions and scheduling texts while you are on a call, then take over before a reply creates a price, product, or delivery commitment.

Reactive replies for one person on one Mac, not automated prospecting.

Keep warm conversations acknowledged

A prospect or customer may text while you are presenting, driving, or handling another account. A simple receipt confirmation can preserve momentum until you have time to check the CRM, price sheet, or product details yourself.

GhostReply watches eligible incoming one-to-one iMessages while it runs on your Mac. It reads local thread history, sends selected relevant context directly to Groq for generation, and auto-sends the reply through Messages. It is useful for low-stakes coordination with people who expect to hear from you, not for cold outreach or unattended selling.

Scope automation to the conversation you know

Start in One person mode with a trusted prospect or customer who is already in an active conversation. Choose the style and reply delay, then monitor the first trial session. A manual reply after GhostReply sends stops the one-person session.

All-contacts mode responds across eligible incoming one-to-one chats. In that mode, your manual message pauses that contact for 30 minutes while other chats may continue. Group chats are skipped. Requirements and setup for sales texts

  1. Use an awake, internet-connected Mac with Messages signed in to the iMessage account you want to monitor.
  2. Install GhostReply in Terminal and grant that terminal app Full Disk Access to read local Messages history.
  3. Grant Messages Automation permission so the terminal can send. Contacts access helps map handles to names.
  4. Add your own Groq API key, select one person or all eligible one-to-one chats, and set a reply style and delay.

History access, the learned profile, configuration, and statistics remain local on your Mac. Selected conversation samples and relevant context go directly from the Mac to Groq for profile and reply generation. Message content does not pass through GhostReply servers. License validation, trial accounting, and analytics data can reach GhostReply services.

The Mac must stay awake, online, and running GhostReply. Close the app to stop all replies. Follow the complete process in the Mac iMessage auto-reply setup guide.

Do not automate claims, commitments, or spam

Keep pricing, discounts, availability, contracts, renewal terms, refunds, security answers, product capabilities, delivery dates, performance guarantees, and any other material promise manual. Do not use GhostReply to impersonate a relationship, create fake urgency, conceal limitations, or send unsolicited messages. It reacts to incoming texts and should only be used where the recipient reasonably expects a response.

GhostReply's urgent and sensitive checks are keyword heuristics, not legal, sales, or messaging compliance controls. They can miss context and make mistakes. Respect consent and opt-outs, follow applicable communication rules, and review the AI iMessage auto-reply safety guide before enabling automatic sending.
